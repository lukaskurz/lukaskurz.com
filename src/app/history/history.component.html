Timeline

  • Birth

    This is where it all begins. The start of my story.

  • Elementary School

    In the rural town of Plainfeld in Salzburg, I went to elementary school.

  • High School

    After moving from Koppl in Salzburg to Wels, I attended the Brucknergymnasium. This is where I had my first contacts with computers, since I went to the "New Media" branch of this school. I learnt about computers and how to use photoshop and the experiences here are also what made me decide to pursue a career in computer science.

  • Technical College

    Having decided to become a coder, I went to the HTL Leonding, where I attend the computer science branch. Being surrounded by like-minded people, I made a lot of friends here and have a lot of positive memories of this place.

  • Internship, eww

    Being a industrious little fella', I immediately wanted to earn some money and put my newly learnt knowledge to use. Sadly there was some misunderstanding regarding my work, which resulted in me doing a lot of clichee intern work, like cleaning the fridge, buying food and so on.

  • Internship, Go-west

    With new hopes of applying my knowledge in computer science, I managed to get an internship at Go-west, a website developing company. I really enjoyed working here, not only because of the beatiful location in the city of Salzburg, but also because I was able to learn a lot about website developing and software engineering in general.

  • Internship, Dynatrace

    Having learned a lot of technologies outside of the school curriculum, I managed to land an internship at one of the bigger tech companies in Linz, Dynatrace. I was very happy to be working on such a cool product like the Dynatrace Agent. I had my first contact with SCRUM, daily standups, version control and other things that are typical for software developing companies nowadays. I also learned about Node.js, which made me appreciate Javascript a bit more, since I had only known it as this weird Language, that had no other use than for frontend developing.

  • Catalysts Coding Contest, Linz

    I participated in the Junior and the normal contest, scoring rank 2(!!!) in the junior and rank 12 in the normal contest. This was a good day.

  • Internship, Catalysts

    My rank in the previous Catalysts Coding Contest, qualified me for an internship at Catalysts, which was one of the other companies I wanted have an intership at. I had heard a lot of positive things about them, which most of of them turned out to be true. I had the unique opportunity to work in a team solely consisting of interns, working on a completely new project. This is was a huge fun and also very interesting, because we had to the whole project management, like having retrospectives, a Product Owner, a demo and a presentation. I also had the opportunity to work as a devop, learning about deployment asweel as debugging and fixing broken deployments.
